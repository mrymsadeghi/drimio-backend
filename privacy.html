Dreamio Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dreamio collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as email and user ID)
  • Dream entries, follow-up answers, and interpretation history
  • Optional profile information (gender, year of birth, recurring dreams and places)
  • Subscription status from Apple (no payment card details)

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide dream journaling and interpretation features
  • Personalize interpretations over time
  • Maintain account access and app reliability
  • Manage subscriptions and restore purchases

3. AI Processing

To provide interpretations, Dreamio sends relevant dream content and context to secure backend and AI services.

AI-generated responses may be inaccurate or incomplete and are for personal reflection only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share data with service providers needed to run Dreamio or where required by law.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting support.

6. Contact

Email: support@drimio.app
