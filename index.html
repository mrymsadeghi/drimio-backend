How it works

Rooted in Jungian dream psychology.

Drimio interprets your dreams through the lens of Carl Jung's depth psychology — the idea that dreams are meaningful messages from the unconscious, woven from personal memory and the deeper, shared language of archetypes like the Shadow, the Anima, and the Self.

Rather than handing you a one-size-fits-all dictionary definition, Drimio treats every symbol as uniquely yours. A snake in your dream doesn't mean what a snake means to anyone else — it means what it has come to mean across your life and your dreams.

The more you log and the more reflective questions you answer, the more Drimio learns the private vocabulary of your subconscious — surfacing patterns, recurring figures, and emotional threads you might not notice on your own.
